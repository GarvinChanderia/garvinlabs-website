# GarvinLabs — Brand Guidelines

> Private reference. Public persona = practitioner/thought leader, not a business or agency.

---

## Identity

**Name:** GarvinLabs
**Positioning:** Omnichannel support automation for D2C brands
**Tone:** Direct, technical, no fluff. Builder who ships, not consultant who advises.
**Voice:** First-person, plain language, specific over vague, results over promises

**What GarvinLabs is publicly:** A practitioner sharing knowledge. Not a company, not an agency.
**What GarvinLabs is privately:** The operating entity behind client work. Inbound/Private Outbound only.

## Content Rules

| Do | Do not |
|---|---|
| Share how systems work | Mention GarvinLabs by name publicly |
| Post anonymised outcomes and metrics | Post rates, packages, or availability |
| Use "I built" not "we offer" | Use founder/consultant/freelancer labels |
| Engage as a practitioner | Run CTAs to hire or book |
| Reference enterprise background | Name Invecto Technologies |

See [[brand-compliance.md]] for full rules.

## Post Design

> Needs templates. Reference `Assets/Content/posts/` for post concepts.

- Card/carousel style: dark background, minimal text, one key insight per frame
- LinkedIn carousel: 6-10 slides, strong hook on slide 1, single takeaway per slide
- X threads: punchy opener, numbered points, no filler
- Reddit: long-form, zero self-promotion framing, knowledge-first
- No generic carousel templates — original layouts only

### The D2C Retention Stack I Build

Three systems, deployed in order of ROI:

**1. Abandoned Cart Recovery (highest ROI)**
Trigger: customer leaves without buying. Response: personalised WhatsApp message within 15 minutes, two follow-ups at 6h and 24h. Shopify + WhatsApp Business API. Most brands go from 1–2% recovery to 4–6% within 30 days.

**2. Post-Purchase Sequence**
Trigger: order confirmed. Response: thank you + usage tips at Day 1, review request at Day 7, reorder nudge at Day 21. WhatsApp + email. Builds repeat purchase habit without any manual effort.

**3. Win-Back Flow**
Trigger: customer hasn't bought in 60+ days. Response: personalised win-back message with their previous order as context, one follow-up at Day 7. Identifies customers before they're gone, not after.

All three run on WhatsApp Business API (official, opt-in, Meta-compliant). Fully automated after setup. Your team sees a Telegram notification when a customer responds — that's the only human touchpoint.

# 🖼️ GarvinLabs: Image Specifications (The Partner)

These prompts generate a mix of approachable technical diagrams and abstract assets using the Brand Green (#10B981) palette.

## 1. Aesthetic Guidelines
*   **Style:** Clean, high-end technical diagrams + macro abstract photography.
*   **Primary Color:** Brand Green (#10B981).
*   **Vibe:** Professional, helpful, modern, human-centric.
*   **Note:** Use the user-provided photo `garvin-architect.jpg` for all personal branding sections.

## 2. Image Prompts

### Asset A: The Triage Wedge (Service Page)
*   **Prompt:** *A clean, high-level technical diagram showing a cluster of messy envelopes (inbound emails) flowing through a glowing green crystalline prism and emerging as neatly organized, color-coded files. Minimalist design, soft shadows, warm neutral background with green accents.*
*   **Usage:** Triage Service Page / The Wedge.

### Asset B: The Collaborative Lab (Demos)
*   **Prompt:** *A close-up of a high-end tablet screen displaying a beautiful, clean n8n automation flow with green status icons. In the background, a soft-focus blurred office environment with warm lighting. Sharp focus on the technology. Professional, helpful vibe.*
*   **Usage:** The Lab / Demo section.

### Asset C: Abstract Connectivity (Backgrounds)
*   **Prompt:** *Abstract digital art featuring soft, flowing lines of green light connecting various geometric nodes. The background is a very light gray with a subtle dot grid. The composition feels light, airy, and organized. Not dark or heavy.*
*   **Usage:** Section backgrounds.

## 3. Brand Consistency
*   **Colors:** Always prioritize #10B981 for highlights.
*   **Feeling:** High clarity and organization.

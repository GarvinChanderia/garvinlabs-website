# 🛸 GarvinLabs Website Brief: Strategic Questions

Garvin, to build a website that functions as a high-conversion sales asset rather than just a digital brochure, please answer the following questions. These will inform the copy, architecture, and design system.

## 1. The Core Offer & Audience
*   **The Hero:** Is the "AI Mail Customer Support Triage" still the primary entry point, or should we position GarvinLabs as a broader "Agentic AI Architecture" firm? AI Mail Customer Support triage is an example of an agentic ai architecture. So agentic ai architecture is the broader category but wedge is ai mail customer support triage.
*   **The Dream Outcome:** For a Support Lead buried in tickets, what is the single most emotional "win" we should highlight? (e.g., "Silence the noise," "Scalability without hiring," "100% triage accuracy"). Single most win is employees can focus on high value tickets and customers get faster responses.
*   **Targeting:** Are we speaking to technical CTOs or non-technical Founders/COOs? (This dictates how much "how it works" vs. "what it does" we show). We should be speaking to non-technical founders and COOs. They are the decision makers for buying the product.

## 2. Brand Voice & Aesthetic
*   **Vibe Check:** For Center Stage, we used "Quiet Luxury." For GarvinLabs, do we want:
    *   **Option A: The Architect (High-rigour, clean, minimal, blueprint-style, monochromatic).**
    *   **Option B: The Futurist (Glows, glassmorphism, dark mode, high-energy, motion-heavy).**
    *   **Option C: The Partner (Human-centric, friendly, approachable, warm-toned, result-focused).**
*   **Color Palette:** Any preferences? (e.g., Deep Space Blue & Neon Teal, or a Neutral "Apple-style" Gray/Silver). Option C, and the primary color to be matching the logo (green).

## 3. Site Architecture & Conversion
*   **Multi-Page Goals:** Beyond the Home and Offer pages, do we need:
    *   A "Learning Library" / Blog? Not yet
    *   Detailed "Case Study" / Demo pages (like the ThreadWave demo)? Yes
    *   A direct "Book a Call" or a "Request a Custom Audit" form? Book a call
*   **Social Proof:** Do we have any testimonials or specific data points (e.g., "reduced triage time by 80%") we can feature? Not yet

## 4. Visuals & Images
*   **Imagery Style:** Do you prefer abstract AI representations (flows, particles), high-end lifestyle (premium workspaces), or technical diagrams/blueprints? Mix of abstract and technical diagrams 
*   **The Founder:** Should your personal brand ([[Garvin]]) be front and center, or should the "Lab" feel like a larger, autonomous entity? Personal brand should be front and center.
